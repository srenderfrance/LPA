At LPA, we have a different approach. It isn't a silver bullet, it isn't magic, it still requires a lot of work. But it is work that anyone can do.

At LPA we use what is called the Growing Participator Approach to language learning. It is a natural, experiential, and participatory method that grows your ability to communicate in Albanian and to understand Albania.
